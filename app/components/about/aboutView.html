Core Values

Do the right thing

We all have choices. We choose to do what is right over the many other options. We focus on quality and service and never cut corners. We support our team members and treat them with respect.

Be passionate in everything we do

We love what we do and our passion drives us to acheive our goals. We strive to set new standards in customer satisfaction and work purposefully to surpass our customers' expectations.

Inspire teamwork

We seek out only the best team players and understand that together - and only together - we accomplish greater things.

Cultivate individual ability, productivity, learning and growth

We nurture our relationships with our team members by supporting and encouraging them to be the best that they can be. We provide the tools and support necessary for individuals to acheive their goals and we never let anyone fall behind.

Build trust and personal responsibility

By using clear and effective communication and setting realistic expectations upfront, we stick by our word and deliver on our prpomises. We accept ownership in our individual responsibilities and understand that each team member's role is critical to the success of the greater Nautilus family.

Be adventurous, open-minded and willing to accept change

Our business is fluid and we are alsways seeking innovative ways to enhance our products and services. We value those who think outside the box and find new ways to develop and enchance the products and services we deliver.

Create a fun, light-hearted environment

It is not work if it is fun! While we are serious about what we do, we try not to take ourselves too seriously. Laughter and smiles are contagious and we have found that happy people are more productive and able to deliver a higher level of customer service.

Do the unexpected and go the extra mile

It is the little things that really count and our team strives to go beyond our clients' and team members' expectations. We ensure that each little detail is accounted for. At Nautilus, we see special things happen every day because our team wants to acheive our mission and understands whil that is important.
