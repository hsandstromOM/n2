Our Story

Founded by Bill Payzant in 2010, Nautilus Company is passionate about improving the lives of our clients. By focusing on long-term solutions, Nautilus reduces the stress of owning or building a home in the South Carolina Lowcountry.

With dedicated teams for both custom homebuilding and home management, Nautilus services the Charleston peninsula, Daniel Island, Sullivan's Island, Isle of Palms and the surrounding areas.

Integrity means doing the right thing, even when no one else is watching. That's why we hold ourselves accountable to our core values in all that we do, whether in the office or on the worksite. We stand by these values: They are exemplified in the people we hire, the people we work with, and in the way in which we do our work.
